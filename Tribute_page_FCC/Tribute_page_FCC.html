Tribute

Eric Clapton was regarded as a quintiessential Blues guitarist, who had his start in John Myall's Bluesbreaks, later replacing Jimmy Page in The Yardbirds. Eric's career experienced tremendous growth after he joined the supergroup, Cream. Cream was a band that was short lived grouping of immensly talented musicians, however it propelled Clapton and his band mates to new found startdom. After leaving Cream Clapton joined "Derek and The Dominos" as a band leader and lead guitarist, shortly after starting his own eduring and legnthy career. Clapton influneced many other musicans and he was giving and charitable, often donating to those in need and offer guidence to his peers and fans who asked for advice at autograph signings and backstage. Eric is regarded as journeyman musician, and he is considered an inspiration for other musicians and listeners alike. His musical legacy will live on forever, however he will be dearly missed by those close to him, most imporantly, his wife and children and fellow muscians who he played alongside.
